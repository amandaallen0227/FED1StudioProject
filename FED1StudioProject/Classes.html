Class Subjects

- Grind & Water -

grinding espresso is a critical element to the cadence of brewing coffee. Whether it is brewed coffee, espresso, chemex, or your home coffee maker: grind matters!

- Espresso Machine Mechanics-

An espresso machine is a complex machine. For the common barista it is the main tool they use to serve their customers. We have offerings at 3 levels to become an expert on servicing your own machine!

- Espresso -

Espresso can be a little touchy. One wrong move and you've messed up your shot! Come learn from the best all the ways to improve your pull.

- Coffee Orgins -

Did you know the only places that coffee beans grow is around the equator? This is where it all starts. If this piece of the process goes wrong it will affect the coffee bean all the way through

- Brewed Coffee -

Drip coffee is the most popular coffee drink option in the world! It is simple yet complex.
